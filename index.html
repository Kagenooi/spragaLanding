UNION LTD

A holding company that sells healthy beverages across Europe. Spraga drinks are freshly brewed and 100% naturally fermented, with 50+ million probiotics in each can.

Spraga was born from a true dedication to a healthy and active lifestyle. Our core values are naturally fermented processes and natural ingredients only. No artificial or unhealthy additives.

Our aim is to to develop unique beverages, finding the right formula, and ensuring affordability for daily consumption. This passion combined with advanced engineering and robust market studies.

PRODUCT CATEGORIES

Kombucha

Is freshly brewed and 100% naturally fermented, ensuring it contains over 50+ billion probiotics per can. It's designed to provide a refreshing and health-boosting experience without the acidic taste often associated with traditional kombucha

Bio Energy Drink

Perfect for those needing a natural energy boost to stay focused and energised throughout the day. Our bio energy drinks are free from unnecessary additives, relying on natural fermentation to deliver a clean and sustained energy lift, especially during afternoons and late evenings.

PRODUCT OFFERS

Our Kombucha Original is a non-flavoured, 100% naturally fermented and slightly carbonated beverage, without vinegar taste. This kombucha is crafted to provide a pure, refreshing taste for the people who are interested in healthy lifestyle.

Energy Boost

Four dynamic flavours - lime & mint, yuzu, fruit punch, and organic cola. These bio energy drinks are formulated to provide a natural energy lift, perfect for staying alert and focused without the crash of traditional energy drinks. Try during afternoon and late night activities!

Everyday Immunity

Additionally we offer the three invigorating flavours - ginger, pomegranate, and cherry. These flavours are chosen for their natural health benefits and delicious taste, making it easy to support your immune system daily.

HOW SPRAGA IS MADE

Our drinks fermentation process is based on three fundamentals:

Natural fermentation of sugar into CO2 and alcohol by wild yeasts

Natural production of lactic acid from yeast fermentation

Natural production of gluconic acid and transformation of alcohol into acetic acid by acetic bacteria

Natural Ingredients

We use only sugar, tea, juices and natural flavours

Advanced Fermentation

Utilising state-of-the-art fermentation techniques to preserve the natural goodness and health benefits of our ingredients.

Production capacity

We have 2 huge factories across Europe: in Ukraine and Czech Republic and now we building the 3d one.

We set an ambitious goal of producing 5 million cans per month on the each factory. This capacity allows us to cater to both the retail sector and HoReCa, meeting wide geographical demands while maintaining a family feel and ensuring paramount quality. Our production capabilities ensure that we can meet the demands of large-scale retailers and distributors across Europe.

OUR ENVIRONMENTAL COMMITMENT

100% Naturally Fermented

No pasteurisation, low sugar content, and no artificial additives ensure our drinks are as healthy as possible.

Sustainable Production

Our facility is equipped with the CO2 recuperation unit which reduces the total CO2 footprint of our factory up to 80% percent. We use advanced automated brewing, cleaning and filling technologies to reduce energy, water, and heating consumption. This commitment reduces human error and maintains consistent, high-quality production, allowing us to produce easily 1.5 million cans of beverages and upwards of 5 million ready-to-sell drinks.

100% Naturally Fermented

We use 100% recyclable aluminium cans, environmentally friendly paints, and recyclable pallets and materials, ensuring our environmental impact is as low as possible. We can meet the demands of large-scale retailers and distributors across Europe

From the start, we partnered with a prominent German institute, helping us validate our idea through a six-month trial. This collaboration ensures our drinks remain high-quality, non-pasteurised, and shelf-stable at up to 25C for more than 180 days.
